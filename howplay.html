How to Play Cake Catching

Objective

Catch as many cakes as possible before the timer runs out!

Gameplay

 Cakes will pop up from the bottom of the screen and move across the screen in an arc.
11.9
Keep an eye on the timer in the center top of the screen, which counts down from 20 seconds.
You scored 0
 Watch your score counter in the top left corner of the screen.

How to Score

Click on a cake to receive points:

  • 1 point is added to your score for each cake caught.
  • 2 seconds are added to your timer for each cake caught.

Increasing Difficulty

The game will get more difficult as time goes on:

  • The speed of the cakes will increase every second.

Good Luck!

Prepare for a challenge and try to get the highest score possible!
